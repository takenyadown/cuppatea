About us.

Founded with a passion for quality and a commitment to authenticity, Cuppa Tea emerged from the dream of creating a haven for tea connoisseurs. Our journey began with a quest for the finest tea leaves sourced from renowned plantations around the world. With meticulous care and a deep appreciation for the craft, we set out to share the art of tea making with those who seek more than just a beverage those who seek an experience.

At the heart of Cuppa Tea is a dedication to craftsmanship and uncompromising quality. Each tea leaf is carefully selected, handpicked, and blended to perfection, ensuring that every cup tells a story of its origin. From classic blends to rare and exotic single-estate teas, our collection reflects the rich tapestry of tea culture worldwide.
